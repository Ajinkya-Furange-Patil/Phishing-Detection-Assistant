═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

WHY THIS WORKS:

Chrome caches content scripts. When you update the extension but 
keep Gmail tabs open, the OLD script stays active. You MUST close 
Gmail tabs BEFORE reloading extension to clear the cache.
